Stage 3: Ideation

In this stage we created sketches and used the ideation process to filter and select our best ideas. We created an affinity diagram and a storyboard to show the scope of our application. Then we constructed a low-fi prototype to demonstrate how our system is to be used, then lastly we conducted a task-centered system Walkthrough of the major user tasks we identified in stage 2.

The tasks we completed this stage are:

  • Affinity Diagram
  • Storyboard
  • Cognitive Walkthrough
  • Low Fidelity Proptotype
