Stage 4: High Fidelity Prototype

In this stage the main goal was to create an interactive high-fidelity prototype. We took into account the functionality, UX design , realism and suitability for usability testing when creating the prototype. To create the prototype we used Adobe XD. After creating the prototype we conducted a heurestic evaluation and improved on the initial prototype design based on our findings.

The tasks we completed this stage are:

  • Describe our updated project idea - task/project management app
  • Constructed a high-fidelity prototype using Adobe XD
  • Performed a Heuristic Evaluation on our prototype
  • Improved on prototype based on evaluation
  • Prepared a report and project presentaion
